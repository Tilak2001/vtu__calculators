About Us

HESDAR Center is a higher education start-up functioning since, 5 September 2017 with a focus for faculty, students and others to provide teaching & learning, skill development, action research and creating modern education workspace, leveraging latest technology and expertise of the board members. it emphasizes on attitude and competencies' development of faculty and not just knowledge up-graduation.

Major focus will be on Evidence based learning outcomes, Bloom's taxonomy, PBL, Curriculum, Assesments and hostilic Feedback system development.

HESDARC, Mysuru has its state of the art Skill Development Center at Hebbal industrial area with about 10,000 sft. of area for training and related activites.

Vision 2025

To be a leading consulting center for faculty training, education based research, skill development, digital transformation and higher education management in India with international quality

VISION 2025

To be a leading consulting center for faculty training, education based research, skill development, digital transformation and higher education management in India with international quality

Board Objectives of HESDARC

Develop and implement scientific and internationally acceptable training modules, Institutional research and effectiveness strategies and processes.

Assist Universities and Institutes to develop, implement, evalute, monitor and continuously modify strategic planning, goals, processes & evaluation indicators.

Support institutes to critically evaluate and implement international educational and training standards and processes.

Conduct need based training programs to selected students, faculty and administration in the area of self & institutional development for recognition & respect.

Focus on evidence based outcomes, PBL, Assesments, Skill development and feedback system.
